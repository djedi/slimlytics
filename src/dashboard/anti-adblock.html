Anti-Adblock Tracking Setup

Why use anti-adblock tracking?

Ad blockers often block analytics scripts from third-party domains. By proxying the tracking through your own domain, you can bypass most ad blockers while still collecting analytics data.

How it works:

This will generate a configuration that uses your site's unique ID as the tracking paths. For example, if your site ID is "abc123", your tracking will use:

  • Script: /abc123.js
  • Beacon: /abc123
  • NoScript: /abc123ns.gif

These random-looking paths are virtually impossible for ad blockers to detect!
